2. Overview

This is a static study hub for subscribers. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics tools. There is no registration, login, or newsletter signup on this site.

3. Legal basis

Where personal data is processed, we rely on Article 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in operating a secure informational website). When you click external links, the third party provider privacy policy applies.

4. Hosting and server logs

The site is served as a static Study Hub via GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc., USA). The hosting provider processes technical data such as IP address, timestamp, requested file, browser type, and operating system. This is required to deliver the site and maintain security. Retention follows the provider policy, typically days to weeks.

GitHub acts as a processor under its contractual privacy terms, including standard contractual clauses for international transfers where applicable. See the GitHub Privacy Statement.

5. Learning progress and notes in your browser

When you use the Study Hub, your browser stores learning data in localStorage on your device only. This includes, for example: lessons marked as read, bookmarks, confidence ratings, focus time, weekly goals, and optional lesson notes you choose to write (plain text with simple formatting).

Notes are voluntary. Do not enter sensitive personal data (for example health data, passwords, or third-party personal data) unless you accept that they remain on your device. We do not read, host, or back up this data on our servers. No name, email, or account is required.

You can export, import, or reset all progress from the Study Hub footer. Individual notes can be cleared in the reader. Import reads a file you choose locally and never uploads it. PDF export of a note uses your browser’s print dialog (Save as PDF) and does not send the note to us. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in a functional learning experience).

Your responsibility for local data. Because this data stays only on your device, you are responsible for backup, export, and protection against loss. Use the Study Hub export function if you want to keep progress or notes. We do not perform server-side backups and cannot restore lost local data.

No guarantee against data loss. Data may be lost when you clear browser data, change devices, update software, encounter technical errors, or stop using the site. We accept no liability for loss of local learning progress or notes, except where mandatory law applies. Intent, gross negligence, and injury to life, body, or health remain unaffected.

7. Local search and Study Assistant

The Study Hub includes sidebar lesson search and a Study Assistant on the Learn page. Both run entirely in your browser. Search queries are not sent to us and are not stored in localStorage. We do not use generative AI, chatbots, or external search APIs. Results are text excerpts from lessons already published on this site, with links to the source lesson.

To show results, your browser loads static files from the same website (for example search-index.json and lesson JSON). Standard hosting server logs described in section 4 may apply to those file requests. We do not operate a separate search backend that logs your query text. Input stays in your browser memory until you leave the page, clear the field, or close the tab. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in helping you navigate the curriculum).
