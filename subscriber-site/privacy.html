2. Overview

This is a static study hub for subscribers. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics tools. There is no registration, login, or newsletter signup on this site.

3. Legal basis

Where personal data is processed, we rely on Article 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in operating a secure informational website). When you click external links, the third party provider privacy policy applies.

4. Hosting and server logs

The site is served as a static Study Hub via GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc., USA). The hosting provider processes technical data such as IP address, timestamp, requested file, browser type, and operating system. This is required to deliver the site and maintain security. Retention follows the provider policy, typically days to weeks.

GitHub acts as a processor under its contractual privacy terms, including standard contractual clauses for international transfers where applicable. See the GitHub Privacy Statement.

5. Learning progress and notes in your browser

When you use the Study Hub, your browser stores learning data in localStorage on your device only. This includes, for example: lessons marked as read, bookmarks, confidence ratings, focus time, weekly goals, and optional lesson notes you choose to write (plain text with simple formatting).

Notes are voluntary. Do not enter sensitive personal data (for example health data, passwords, or third-party personal data) unless you accept that they remain on your device. We do not read, host, or back up this data on our servers. No name, email, or account is required.

You can export, import, or reset all progress from the Study Hub footer. Individual notes can be cleared in the reader. Import reads a file you choose locally and never uploads it. PDF export of a note uses your browser’s print dialog (Save as PDF) and does not send the note to us. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in a functional learning experience).

6. Local search and Study Assistant

The Study Hub includes sidebar lesson search and a Study Assistant on the Learn page. Both run entirely in your browser. Search queries are not sent to us and are not stored in localStorage. We do not use generative AI, chatbots, or external search APIs. Results are text excerpts from lessons already published on this site, with links to the source lesson.

To show results, your browser loads static files from the same website (for example search-index.json and lesson JSON). Standard hosting server logs described in section 4 may apply to those file requests — but we do not operate a separate search backend that logs your query text. Input stays in your browser memory until you leave the page, clear the field, or close the tab. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in helping you navigate the curriculum).

7. No tracking cookies

This site does not set marketing or analytics cookies. We do not use fingerprinting or profiling.

8. Fonts

Fonts are self hosted on our domain. No connection to Google Fonts is made when pages load.

9. Monetization on third-party platforms

The Study Hub is free and does not show ads. Revenue from online content may come from third-party platforms, notably YouTube (AdSense) and Facebook. That monetization happens only on those platforms, not on this website.

For data processing related to ads, accounts, or analytics on YouTube or Facebook, only Google’s and Meta’s privacy policies apply. If you use those services, review their cookie and profiling practices there.

10. External links

Links to GitHub, YouTube, and Facebook open only after you click. We do not embed YouTube or Facebook players or load preview images from their servers. After you click, the third party provider’s privacy policy applies. Linked destinations may change over time; we do not continuously monitor external pages.

11. Your rights

You have the rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and portability where applicable under GDPR. Contact nabidinmotionofficial@gmail.com. You may lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, for example in Berlin, Germany.

Because progress and notes stay on your device, you can usually delete them yourself at any time (clear a note, use Reset progress, or clear site data in your browser settings) without contacting us.

12. Content updates

Curriculum content is synced from GitHub and may change at any time. This does not involve personal data processing on our servers because we do not operate user accounts. Liability and change notices are set out in our Terms of Use and Nutzungsbedingungen (DE).
