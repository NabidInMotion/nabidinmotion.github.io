Security Acknowledgments

Responsible disclosure · Nabid In Motion Study Hub

If you believe you have found a security issue affecting the Nabid In Motion Study Hub (nabidinmotion.com), please report it responsibly. We appreciate researchers who help keep learners safe.

Report a vulnerability

Email privacy@nabidinmotion.com with a clear description, steps to reproduce, and impact assessment. Please do not publish details before we have had a reasonable time to investigate.

Scope

  • This static study hub site and its hosted content
  • Configuration or deployment issues that affect user safety or privacy

Out of scope: third-party services (GitHub, YouTube), social engineering, denial-of-service tests against production without prior agreement, and issues in the open curriculum repository unless they directly compromise this hosted site.

What to expect

We aim to acknowledge valid reports within a few business days. We may ask for clarification. With your permission, we will credit researchers on this page after a fix or accepted mitigation.
